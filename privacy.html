Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. Introduction

VoiceNote ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application that transcribes voice messages and audio. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the app.

2. Data we process

To provide transcription and summaries, we send the audio you select (or a copy of it) to third-party services that perform speech-to-text and text processing. Processed text (transcript, summary) is stored on your device.

  • Audio: Sent to our transcription provider (e.g. OpenAI) only when you request transcription.
  • Transcripts and summaries: Stored locally on your device.
  • Subscription status: Stored locally; we do not operate a user account server.

3. Third-party services

We use third-party services (such as OpenAI) for transcription and AI features. Their use of data is governed by their respective privacy policies. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data on your device

Audio files, transcripts, and app data are stored on your device. You can delete all data using the "Delete all transcribers" option in the app settings.

5. Children

VoiceNote is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: support@voicenote.app
